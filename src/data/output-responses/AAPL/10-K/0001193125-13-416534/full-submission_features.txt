Financial Growth and Trends
---------------------------

During the past fiscal year (2015), Apple Inc. reported its highest-ever revenue and net income. compared to the prior year, total revenue grew +13.0% and net income grew +409.3%. Both revenue and net income outperformed industry averages.

Gross margin was a standout metric for Apple in 2015, reaching an impressive +39.3%. This was an improvement from 2014's gross margin rate of +38.3%. Gross margin exceeded industry averages by 7.0% and 6.8% for 2015 and 2014, respectively.
