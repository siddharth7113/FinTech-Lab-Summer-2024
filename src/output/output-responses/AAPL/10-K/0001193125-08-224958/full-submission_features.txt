 Table of Contents Note 1: Summary of Significant Accounting Policies that most interest me is that the company has been using segment reporting in accordance with SFAS No. 131, which dictates certain disclosures about segments of its business. The segments discussed include Retail, Service, Software, Product, Facility Management, Professional Services, On-line, Corporate, Interest Income, Program Development, Geographic, and Other Income. The company also reported Net Sale revenue for each segment as well as Operating Income. Additionally, the company states that it uses depreciation and amortization on its property and equipment assets, and that these assets are depreciated over either a 3-5 year period. Finally, the company notes that it uses a conservative accounting approach to several items, including development costs.
